Strategic Ask
The question you leave in the room.
This is the last move. Ask it after the diagnostic statement has landed. It names the gap without labeling the company.
Primary strategic question

Which AI initiative, if any, is designed to improve outcomes for both sides of the business simultaneously?

If they name one

Ask what data from the other participant type flows into that initiative as a primary input. If they hesitate, the initiative is likely scoped to one side only. That is your confirmation.

If they cannot name one

The gap has been located. Do not press. Move to articulating what filling it would require, and use the confirmed gaps to name the specific structural absences the commissioning brief would need to address.
